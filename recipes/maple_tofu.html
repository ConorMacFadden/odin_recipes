Maple Glazed Tofu

We love good Maple Soy Glazed Tofu and this deliciously simple, chef-curated recipe doesn't disappoint. Time to bust out the pots and pans!

Ingredients

  • Tofu
  • Plain Flour
  • Sesame Seeds
  • Basmati Rice
  • Soy Sauce
  • Star Anise
  • Tenderstem Broccoli
  • Pak Choy
  • Thai Style Spice Blend
  • Garlic Clove
  • Red Chilli
  • Spring Onion
  • Maple Syrup
  • Lime
  • Ginger Puree

Instructions

  1. Chop the tofu, coat with the plain flour and fry for approx. 8 minutes.

  2. Cook the rice with the star anise.

  3. Mix the maple syrup with some soy sauce. Once the tofu has cooked reduce to medium heat and add the maple soy mix. Cook for a further 2 minutes to glaze. Once cooked place in a bowl for later.

  4. While the maple is cooking chop the tenderstem broccoli, chilli, and peel and grate the garlic.

  5. Fry the broccoli in for 2 minutes until starting to brown at the edges then add a splash of water and cover with a lid to steam fry for 2 minutes or until softened.

  6. Add teh pak choy, garlic, chilli and ginger puree to the frying pan with the broccoli and fry for a further 2 minutes.

  7. Remove the star anise from the rice then add the rice to the frying pan. Add some soy sauce and a squeeze of lime juice and mix the rice through with the fried veg.

  8. Serve the veg in bowls then top with the glazed tofu.
