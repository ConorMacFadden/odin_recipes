Couscous Stuffed Peppers

Simple roasted peppers filled with couscous, cashew nuts, mozzarella and herbs.

Ingredients

  • Red Bell Peppers
  • Mozzarella
  • Spring Onions
  • Couscous
  • Italian Herb Mix
  • Cashew nuts

Instructions

  1. Cut the peppers in half and de-seed.

  2. Place the peppers in an oven safe dish and place in a preheated oven at 200 degrees C for 15 minutes.

  3. Pour the couscous into a bowl and mix in the Italian herbs. Add boiling water and leave to cook for 5 minutes.

  4. Chop the spring onions and mozzarella. Once the couscous is cooked mix in the chopped spring onion and mozzarella.

  5. When the peppers have been roasting for 15 minutes stuff them with the couscous mix and return to the oven for another 15 minutes.

  6. Serve and enjoy! Goes well with a freshly baked bread roll and some olive oil and balsamic vinegar.
