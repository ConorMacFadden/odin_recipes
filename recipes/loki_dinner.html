Loki's Dinner

Loki might be a heckin dog but he's not too picky when it comes to food. He likes his crunchy kibble with a bit of tastier meat in gravy.

Ingredients

  • Hills Science Plan Dog Food
  • Naturo with beef and gravy
  • All bran

Instructions

  1. Put some kibble in a bowl.

  2. Add a small sprinkle of all bran because he needs the extra fibre.

  3. Top with 1/4 tin of meaty chunks in gravy.

  4. Tell him to sit because he should have good manners, then place the bowl down and let him enjoy his dinner.
